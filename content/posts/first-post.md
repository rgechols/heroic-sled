This is a sample post to verify the site is working. It has a title, a date, tags, and enough body text to test the reading time calculation and summary truncation.

The site is built with Hugo and designed to serve two kinds of content: longer essays like this one, and short micro posts that appear without titles. Both feed into a single timeline on the home page, but they have distinct visual treatments and separate feeds.
